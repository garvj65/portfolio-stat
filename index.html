About Me

I’m the type who gets excited by new tech and ends up turning side projects into late-night experiments. From full-stack apps to playful AI integrations, I enjoy pushing the limits of what I can build and learn. You’ll often find me working with Next.js, Node.js, and Tailwind CSS, or exploring the power of AI tools and APIs to create smarter, more intuitive applications. I’m a big believer in learning by doing. Whether it’s hacking together a fun weekend project, building out a polished portfolio app, or figuring out how to connect a database at 2 a.m., I’m always chasing that “aha!” moment. When I'm not coding, I’m probably reading novels, sketching out a new idea, or wondering if I could build a better way to organize my life with tech.
